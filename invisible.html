The Invisible Man

Author - H.G Wells


summary - The story centres around Dr. Jack Griffin, a young scientist who has discovered the secret of invisibility and has taken the drug responsible for the incredible feat. The drug, however, also drives the doctor mad. He ends up terrorizing a quaint British village and eventually commits murder.
